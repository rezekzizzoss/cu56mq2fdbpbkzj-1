<p><p>Candied and semi-candied fruits, often used in baking and confectionery, are fruits that have been preserved by soaking or boiling in sugar syrup, resulting in a sweetened, chewy product. Candied fruits are completely saturated with sugar, while semi-candied fruits maintain some moisture, offering a balance between sweetness and texture. These products are popular in various culinary applications, including desserts, salads, and snacks.</p><p>The Candied and Semi Candied Fruit Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.10% during the forecast period. Health-conscious trends are also influencing market dynamics, with manufacturers introducing healthier options, such as organic and low-sugar variations. Furthermore, expanding online retail channels and globalization are enhancing product availability. Innovations in packaging and preservation techniques are making these products more appealing to consumers, while the growing trend of exotic fruits is expanding the variety offered in the market.
