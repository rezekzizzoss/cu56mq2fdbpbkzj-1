<p><strong>The Commercial Organic Bread Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Machine Mill Flour</li><li>Stone Mill Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial organic bread flour market includes two primary types: machine mill flour and stone mill flour. Machine mill flour is produced using high-speed roller mills, resulting in finely ground flour with consistent texture and quality, often favored for mass production. In contrast, stone mill flour is made through traditional grinding methods with stone mills, retaining more nutrients and flavor due to lower heat generation. This type is preferred by artisanal bakers seeking authenticity and uniqueness in their bread products.</p></p>
