<p><strong>The Water Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw Chillers</li><li>Centrifugal Chillers</li><li>Reciprocating Chillers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water chiller market encompasses several types based on their operating mechanisms. Screw chillers use rotating screws to compress refrigerant, efficiently providing cooling for medium to large applications. Centrifugal chillers utilize centrifugal force to circulate refrigerant at high capacities, ideal for large-scale operations. Reciprocating chillers employ pistons to compress refrigerant, suited for smaller applications with varying cooling demands. Other market types may include thermoelectric, absorption, and hybrid chillers, catering to specific needs and energy efficiency requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
